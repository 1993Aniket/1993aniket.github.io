PSU

MAHANAGAR GAS LIMITED

The Challenges

  • Flat operating Margins in the past 5 years
  • Benchmarking of MGL offer with the success of Indraprastha Gas
  • Messaging with media was very limited to managing brand reputation during IPO phase
  • Financial details were restricted to DRHP, no way-forward statements allowed
  • Dependence of revenue majorly on one segment i.e. the CNG business.

The PR Strategy

  • Organise relationship meetings with leading publications and key media personnel
  • Highlight backing of a strong parent, GAIL, BG group and Government of Maharashtra
  • To project the positive operational highlights of the company ahead of their IPO
  • To highlight the profitability of MGL which is better than its peers
  • Built trust and credibility for the brand

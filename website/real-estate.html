REAL ESTATE

K RAHEJA CORP

The brief

  • Showcase K Raheja Corp as a strong, governance driven organisation
  • Showcase Artesia and Vivarea as residences which live up to its commitment of ‘uultra-luxury living’
  • Highlight Vistas as luxury residential complexes, congregating like minded people to live together in harmony, offering the perfect living space
  • Feature Viva as the perfect 2nd home | holiday home with vast green expanse
  • Emphasize on the productive and stimulating state of the art work environments at Mindspace & Commerzone
  • Focus on the company’s Green initiatives and environmental certifications
  • Showcase K Raheja as an equal opportunity employer and high on performance-culture
  • Plan and execute different CSR initiatives undertaken by K Raheja Corp

Objectives

  • Corporate
  • To showcase K Raheja Corp’s diverse extensions, focusing on successful formats, future business plans and projections
  • Commercial
  • To consolidate position in the market as the first preference for office requirements in the commercial space
  • Residential
  • To reinforce K Raheja Corp’s position as a premium luxury and one of the most trusted real estate developer
  • Sustainability
  • Drive awareness for green homes, delivering a safer, cleaner future
  • HR
  • To profile as an equal opportunities company, offering the best opportunities to the best talent
  • CSR
  • Imagery of a good Corporate Social Citizen with a positive impact on the society K Raheja Corp operates in.

Challenges

  • No media exposure before Concept PR came on board
  • Unavailability of promoters to meet financial and business media in keys cities of Mumbai, Pune and Hyderabad
  • Regulatory changes in the real estate industry
  • Compliance with the editorial changes with respect to real estate media

The PR Strategy

  • Advisory on K Raheja Corp and its offerings shared with all media
  • Organized meetings for spokesperson/s with key media
  • Conducted press briefings, conferences and PR events
  • Contributed towards industry trends and latest reports
  • Participation in industry stories & thought leadership opportunities
  • Highlight various accomplishments of the Company

The Result

  • Successfully projected K Raheja Corp, who has been instrumental in changing the landscape of Mumbai realty market through world class quality products, performance, delivery and new innovation
  • Highlighted K Raheja Corp’s green initiatives and environmental certifications
  • Guided & handled crisis as per a strategic crisis communications plan
  • Continued to sustain awareness and enhance the brand equity of the Company
  • Ensured brand recall among real estate media and influencers

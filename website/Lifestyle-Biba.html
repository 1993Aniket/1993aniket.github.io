LifeStyleCase Study

Biba

About the Digital Film

  • The #ChangeIsBeautiful digital film was all about welcoming and standing for the change in the Indian context of marriage

The Thought

  • Address the societal biases of arranged marriages in India
  • Embrace and celebrate change in the Indian context of marriage

PR Brief

  • Client wanted the agency to create exposure and conversations around the new film to drive home the connect of the brand with the film as well as emphasize the new age advertising and new trends being followed by brands

    • The Strategy

  • A press release on the digital film across media across India
  • Exclusive interaction of Mr. SiddharthBindra with ET Times, NDTV and AFAQS (one of the key agenda of the client)
  • Marketing supplement such as Brand Equity and Brand Wagon targeted for a detailed story

Execution

  • Agency approached selected bloggers/online influencers before the ad film digital launch
  • Film was launched on the evening of 29th December
  • PR team worked very closely with the digital team with online contest run on Facebook simultaneously to keep the thrust alive
  • Apart from this, Agency took the new approach of Seeding the article and changing the headline to "You Won't Believe What Happens How This Arranged Marriage Meeting Turned Out!", which was well-received and picked by marketing sites like ScoopWoop

OutCome

  • Within a few hours of the film being aired digitally, it went viral
  • Over 1 million views generated in the first 19 hours of its release, which went upto 14 million
  • Reports, trend stories and interviews with the spokesperson carried by-
    • Leading publications such as The Economic Times, The Financial Express
    • Trade publications like afaqs, AdAge etc
    • Electronic channels like NDTV-All About Ads, Zee Business-Brands and CNN IBN-Storyboard
  • Article with headline "You Won't Believe What Happens How This Arranged Marriage Meeting Turned Out!", picked by marketing sites like ScoopWoop
  • BIBA now seen as a brand which creates awareness around the gaps which exist in our system, able to make emotional connect with the TG, bringing them closer to the brand
  • The overall campaign generated the desired impact and was well-appreciated by the TG, consumers and media, as well by the industry

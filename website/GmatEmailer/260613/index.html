HIGHLIGHT OF THE MONTH

The Supreme Court of India has approved the ratification of GMAT for use
in 2013-14 admissions to PGDM programs offered at business schools in India,
regulated by AICTE.
 

The Supreme Court order will now help support Indian business schools in finding
management education aspirants from India and across the world for their PGDM
programmes. Candidates will have the option of sending their GMAT scores to apply
for admission to PGDM programmes at leading Indian business schools,
offering them more choice in accessing quality graduate management education.

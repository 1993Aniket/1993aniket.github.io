BRAND COMMUNICATION

MCNROE CONSUMERS PVT LTD

McNROE- is the parent company of famous deodorant brands like Wild Stone and Secret Temptation, headquartered in Kolkata.

The Challenges

  • While the company had a strong market share and holds the second position in fragrance category ,yet was not known amongst the consumers and media
  • The sub-brand Wild Stone being bigger than the parent company itself
  • Media was unaware of the man behind the company- Mr. N K Daga
  • Not much knowledge about different variants

Our Approach

  • Selective media briefings, one-on-one interactions with Mr. Daga across Kolkata, Delhi, Mumbai
  • Breakthrough Forbes India story, which identified McNROE as one of the leading players in the fragrance industry
  • This was followed by multi-city press tour introducing the revamped Secret Temptation deodorants, a new identity and brand new Tag Line
  • Prominent interactions with PTI, Hindu Business Line, ET retail etc. to increase brand visibility
  • Blogger engagement in the hometown Kolkata during the launch, to spread awareness on digital platform and weave conversation around new tagline of Secret Temptation “ Own your Temptation”, prior to the launch of new TVC

Blogger Engagement on Valentine’s day

  • A digital activity around the valentine’s Week
  • Tied up with lifestyle bloggers who have younger audience
  • Bloggers received a hamper from Secret Temptation, and concealed the name of the brand, asking their followers who can the #TheSecretAdmirer
  • Revealing after few days that Secret temptation is their #SecretAdmirer
  • Followers/Readers have to comment with their guesses
  • The first 3 correct answers, won a hamper from the brand
  • The activity was a huge success digitally, gathered tons of eyeballs and the contest was well received by the young audience
